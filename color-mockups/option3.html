Why This Adjustment?

The shift from #d4a017 to #d4a520 is subtle but intentional. By increasing the green channel slightly (0x17 → 0x20), the mustard becomes 2% warmer and 3% lighter. This creates a friendlier, slightly more approachable tone while maintaining the sophisticated gold aesthetic. The change is barely perceptible but psychologically shifts the color from "precise industrial" to "warm professional."
